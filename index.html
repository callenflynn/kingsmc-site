Server Rules

  • Respect Everyone: No bullying, hate, spam, or inappropriate behavior.
  • No Griefing: Don’t destroy or alter others’ builds.
  • No Stealing: Don’t take items without permission.
  • No Hacking: No cheats, X-ray, or unfair mods.
  • PvP: Must be consensual.
  • No Duping: No exploits or glitches.
  • Build Respectfully: Avoid offensive or too-close builds.
  • Lag Control: Farms must be efficient and have off-switches.
  • World Cleanup: No floating trees or mess.
  • Contracts: Use signed books. Break = temp ban.
  • Staff: No abuse. Must provide proof for bans.
  • Bots: Must not disrupt gameplay.
